About Me

Hi! My name is Colter Giem, and I'm a frontend dev, font designer and vector artist based in Denver, Colorado. I'm experienced in visual design, web design and mainly just design in general. My goal is to create an awesome visual experience for my clients, and I hope to use the newest technologies and techniques to make even the most persnickety client happy with my work. You can email me here or check out my Dribbble and Github. Or, if you're not feeling up to checking out my stuff there, you're welcome to visit my site and look at my work firsthand. Anyway, this is my inaugural A Thing A Week entry. Hope you like it!
